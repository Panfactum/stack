## Are You At Risk?

- Do you run unsandboxed agents AND do any of the following:
  - Store secrets unencrypted on-disk (e.g., `.env`, `.kube/config`, `.aws/credentials`)? — <span style={{color: '#f97066'}}>PWNed</span>
  - Run local processes with credentials in environment variables? — <span style={{color: '#f97066'}}>PWNed</span>
  - Give your user access to privileged system services (docker, journal, etc.)? — <span style={{color: '#f97066'}}>PWNed</span>
  - Can your user access the internet? - Believe it or not,<span style={{color: '#f97066'}}> PWNed</span>
