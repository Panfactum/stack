## Upgrade Modules in Order

This release changes the way that public ingress TLS certificates are provisioned in order to avoid
hitting rate limits on large clusters. **This architectural update requires that the modules be upgraded in
the following order:**

1. [`kube_cert_issuers`](/docs/edge/reference/infrastructure-modules/direct/kubernetes/kube_cert_issuers)

2. [`kube_ingress_nginx`](/docs/edge/reference/infrastructure-modules/direct/kubernetes/kube_ingress_nginx).
   **To avoid service disruptions, you MUST wait until all the old NGINX pods have been fully terminated
   before proceeding.**

3. The remainder of the modules may be updated in any order.
